## Page Type Conventions

- `source`: per-source English summary page in `wiki/sources/`
- `entity`: named model, project, tool, paper lineage, or organization
- `concept`: reusable idea, method, or technical theme
- `comparison`: explicit cross-source comparison
- `overview`: orienting map of a topic area
- `synthesis`: higher-level conclusion that integrates multiple pages
